Virat Kohli

Indian Cricket Legend — Test Jersey No.18



Virat Kohli on Wikipedia

Major Achievements

  • 123 Test Matches, 9,230 runs, 30 centuries
  • Holds record for most double centuries in Tests by an Indian (7)
  • Most successful Indian Test captain with 40 wins from 68 matches
  • First Asian captain to win a Test series in Australia (2018-19)
  • Four-time ICC Test Mace winner as No.1 Test Team Captain
  • ICC Cricket World Cup winner (2011); Champions Trophy (2013); T20 World Cup (2024); U-19 World Cup (2008)
  • Over 80 international centuries (second only to Sachin Tendulkar)
  • Fastest ever to 8,000, 9,000, 10,000, 11,000, 12,000 ODI runs
  • Most ODI centuries: 51, surpassing Sachin Tendulkar
  • Most Player of the Series awards in T20Is (7)
  • Scored 3,000+ runs in ICC white-ball events
  • Highest run-getter of the 2023 ODI World Cup, also first with 700+ runs in a single edition
  • Sir Garfield Sobers Trophy (ICC Cricketer of the Year): 2017, 2018
  • ICC Men’s Cricketer of the Decade (2010–2020)
  • Padma Shri (2017), Arjuna Award (2013), Rajiv Gandhi Khel Ratna (2018)
